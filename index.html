About Us

At Adcircles Productions , we believe that every space tells a story. Established in 2012 in the heart of Nairobi, we specialize in transforming homes, residences and commercial interior spaces into elegant, functional environments that reflect your unique style and needs.

With over a decade of experience, our team blends creativity with practicality, ensuring every project is both beautiful and livable.

Our Services

  • Residential Design:From cozy apartments to luxury villas, we craft spaces that feel like home.
  • Commercial Interiors: Offices, retail spaces and hospitality environments designed to impress and perform.
  • Space Planning:Smart layouts that maximize flow, functionality and comfort.
  • Color & Material Consultation:Expert guidance on palettes, textures and finishes.
